Directions

From the South (Burlington, Woburn):

Take 93 North to Exit# 44A (Route 495 North towards Lawrence). Take Exit# 41A (Route 28 South toward Andover). Follow 1/8 of a mile. The Courtyard Place is on the left, just before the junction of Route 133.

From the North (Salem, NH):

Take 93 South to Exit# 44A (Route 495 North towards Lawrence). Take Exit# 41A (Route 28 South towards Andover) Follow 1/8 of a mile. The Courtyard Place is on the left, just before the junction of Route 133

From the West (Chelmsford, Lowell):

Take 495 North to Exit# 41A (Route 28 South towards Andover). Follow 1/8 of a mile. The Courtyard Place is on the left, just before the junction of Route 133

From the East (Newburyport, Haverhill, Lawrence):

Take 495 South to Exit# 41 (Route 28 Lawrence and Andover). Stay straight on Route 28 South towards Andover. Follow 1/8 of a mile. The Courtyard Place is on the left, just before the junction of Route 133
